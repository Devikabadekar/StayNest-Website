Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a room on StayNest?

You can book a room by searching for available rooms, selecting one, and following the steps for payment and confirmation. Make sure your account is verified to complete the process.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, bookings can be canceled from your account dashboard. Please note that cancellation policies vary depending on the host's terms.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and net banking for payments. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your safety.
